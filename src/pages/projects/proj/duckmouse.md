## Production

After we were more or less done with the site, we needed to get ahold of the actual computermice. After some reverse image searching and resurch we found the manufacturer on Alibaba. They smalest quanta they could manifature was 500, so that was the number we went with. Some would prehaps say this sounded a bit stupid as 500 is a lot. But some also say the earth is flat, so there is that.

## Entrepenuship
A big part of donig this project was learning about how one could go about selling a product. We tried to read upp on what we were legaly obliged to do and how to manage the income, expenses and so on. We figured that if we sold for less than 50,000nok in a year we were not obigated to pay VAT, but our earnings would obviusly be taxed as with a regular job. We bolth registred an "enkeltpersonforetak" and taxed thugh that.

Later that summer the boxes of computer mice apeared. When they arived at the door we started to understand the true scope of what we had done. It turnes out that 500 hundred of annything is rather a lot.

## Sales
We now only had one isue left. Selling theese mice. We started with quite a few sales to friends and friends of friends. We bought some stickers and gave them with the mouse. It quicly became apparent that even though I had found the prosess of building the website and buying the mice to be fun, selling them was mutch more tidius. Isak had quite a few good ideas on marketing, but we sadly never came round to do it. As of wrighting this post we have sold 48 of the total 500 mice.

There sadly apeard one tiny tiny problem. The mice have all started to leak oil. It turnes out that making coputer eqipment with oil inside is not a great idea. The mice were not well sealed and we slowly began understandng why nonoe else sold theese mice. We have tried to stop the leaking with a viraiaty of glues, but as of now we have found no fix. Duckmouse is therefore for now over and shut down. However the website is still upp, and who knows. Perhaps we figure out how to fix them or maby we find another usage for them all together.
