blurb: The Brothers Karamazov is the eighth and final novel by Russian author Fyodor Dostoevsky. Dostoevsky spent nearly two years writing The Brothers Karamazov, which was published as a serial in The Russian Messenger from January 1879 to November 1880. Dostoevsky died less than four months after its publication. It has been acclaimed as one of the supreme achievements in world literature. The Brothers Karamazov is a passionate philosophical novel that discusses questions of God, free will, and morality.
